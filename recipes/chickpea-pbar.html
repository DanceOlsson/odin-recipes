Peanut Butter Chickpea Protein Bar

Description

These peanut butter chickpea protein bars are a great, nutritious snack packed with plant-based protein and fiber. They're easy to make and are perfect for a post-workout boost or a quick on-the-go treat!

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas (about 400 g), drained
  • 2 dl oats
  • 0.6 dl allulose
  • 4 scoops vanilla protein powder (about 60 g)
  • 1 dl peanut butter
  • ½ dl aquafaba (chickpea water)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 175°C (350°F).
  2. In a food processor, combine the chickpeas, oats, allulose, vanilla protein powder, peanut butter, and aquafaba. Blend until you achieve a smooth and even mixture.
  3. Press the mixture firmly into a lined baking dish to form an even layer.
  4. Bake for about 20–25 minutes or until the bars are firm to the touch.
  5. Let the bars cool completely before cutting them into squares. Enjoy your protein-packed snack!
